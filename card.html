Sloth

The three-toed sloth can turn its head almost all the way around!

  • Scientific name: Bradypus variegatus
  • Average Length: 45 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 27 years
  • Habitat: Tropical forests

Sloths are the worlds slowest animals, and they are quite the introwert type. These solitary creatures rarely interact with one another outside of breeding season.
